About

A journey from enterprise software to startup building

My career has been defined by building and scaling teams, products, and businesses across multiple geographies and industries. Starting in 2001 with IT consulting at Neilsoft in Pune, I transitioned to enterprise software at Autodesk in 2006, where I coordinated 150+ engineers across internal teams and global vendor partners spanning Japan, China, India, Ukraine, Russia, Switzerland, US, Vietnam, Philippines, and Argentina.

In 2013, the startup ecosystem in Southeast Asia had moved beyond its nascent stage—exciting markets were opening up for building, and I took the plunge. Since then, my entire career has been focused on building and scaling startup ventures—from Mobikon's restaurant CRM platform that scaled to 6,000+ restaurants globally, to Subtra's fintech solution for B2B spend management.

Today, I work with Binny Bansal's family office, 3 State Ventures, driving innovation incubation and technology due diligence. I'm based in Sydney but maintain a rigorous travel schedule between Singapore and Bangalore to stay connected with our portfolio companies.

Founder Mindset + Operator Discipline

The full 0→1→10 journey: from idea validation and scrappy MVPs on a shoestring, to scaling teams, crafting go-to-market strategies, building product roadmaps, hiring leadership, and raising venture capital. Comfortable in the trenches and the boardroom.

Outsourcing expertise from both sides—as vendor (Neilsoft) delivering to US clients, and as principal (Autodesk) managing global vendor partners. Built distributed startup orgs with HQ in Singapore/Sydney and engineering teams in India.

Investment & Due Diligence
